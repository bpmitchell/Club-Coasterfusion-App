    Club CoasterFusion Information:

    About Us

    Club CoasterFusion was established in November of 2009 with a few key priorities in mind. The first and most important priority is you! We want to make everyone feel welcome when they register and become part of Club CoasterFusion. Whether your a seasoned roller coaster veteran or a newcomer to the scene, everyone is equal! Our number two priority is of course the price one pays to become part of our club! We want to make sure you get the best out of your membership, so why charge astronomical membership fees. Why not save some money!?

    Membership Cost

    Club CoasterFusion is the world's cheapest roller coaster enthusiast organization. We offer three different types of memberships that can be purchased by anyone from anywhere on the planet:

    United States Memberships: $20.00 (Plus $5 each additional member, up to 4 allowed)

    Canada and Mexico Memberships: $25.00 (Plus $5 each additional member, up to 4 allowed)

    International Memberships: $30.00 (Plus $5 each additional member, up to 4 allowed)

    When making your purchase you will have the option to add up to four additional members. You will act as the primary membership holder. Therefore all important club information and etc will be transmitted through you.

    Code of Conduct

    Club CoasterFusion has partnered with the parks listed for discounts and events. However, Club CoasterFusion is not responsible for those persons who decide to act out in ways that may harm themselves or others. Therefore, by registering for Club CoasterFusion you agree to the following Codes of Conduct:

    - 1 - It is my responsibility to follow posted and verbal safety rules at all times at all theme parks partnered with Club CoasterFusion without exception, including (including but not limited to) not attempting to circumvent the safety features of the ride.

    - 2 - I understand that some rider behaviors are so obviously unsafe that posting them is not necessary, and so I agree that I will use caution and conservative judgement in riding.

    - 3 - I understand that failure to follow these Codes of Conduct may result in me and my group being escorted from the park and or banned from the respected theme park and Club CoasterFusion.

    - 4 - I understand that my Club CoasterFusion Membership will expire on December 31st of the given year. This is also stated in your welcome packet as well as on the back of your Club CoasterFusion Membership Card.

    - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

    Club CoasterFusion has the right to suspend or ban club member(s) who have been found guilty of violating these Codes of Conduct. If banned Club CoasterFusion will request Membership Card. If suspended Club CoasterFusion will notify each theme park of members name and ID number. If suspended or banned member is stripped of all benefits.
